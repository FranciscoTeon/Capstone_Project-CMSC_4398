About us

New Horizons Flooring LLC, established in 2024, is a family-owned business with over 8 years of experience in the flooring industry, specializing in residential flooring solutions. Based in Arizona, we focus on the installation of laminate, luxury vinyl plank (LVP), and hardwood floors. Our mission is to deliver top-quality flooring with meticulous installation and outstanding customer satisfaction. We are dedicated to transforming homes with durable, beautiful, and comfortable flooring. Our team values client relationships, offering personalized service and guidance throughout the process to ensure a smooth, stress-free experience. We aim to enhance your home with flooring that combines style, comfort, and longevity.
