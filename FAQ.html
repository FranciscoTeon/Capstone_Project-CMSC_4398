Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you will need to know for your next project.

Where do you service?

We service all of Maricopa county along with San Tan Valley and Casa Grande.

How do you price jobs?

We take into accont the type of material, the square footage of installtion area, and the type of existing floor that needs to be removed.

Can you install my new floor over existing floor?

Yes, we certainly can install your new floor over existing floor, however, there might be more preperation needed which can inquire extra charges.

Do you only demo. tile flooring?

No, tile is not the only floor we remove, we are also able to demo. laminate, lvp, hardwood, sheet vinyl, and a variety of other existing floors.

What can I do to prepare for my new floor?

I would be of great help if you removed fragile items from installtion areas and removing books or nick nacks from shelves can also assist us in the installtion process.

Do you move furniture?

Yes, we move heavy furniture to clear the installtion area, items like safes, pianos/organs, and pool tables will inquire an extra charge to be moved.
