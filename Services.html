Services

Luxury Vinyl Plank

Our Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP) installation service offers a stylish, durable, and cost-effective flooring solution for any space. Designed to mimic the look of hardwood, LVP is water-resistant, easy to maintain, and perfect for high-traffic areas. With a variety of designs and finishes, our expert team ensures a seamless installation, providing you with a floor that combines beauty and practicality for years to come.

Laminate

Give your home a fresh, stylish look with our laminate installation service. We offer durable, beautiful laminate floors that bring the charm of wood without the worry. Affordable and easy to maintain, our expert team ensures a smooth, flawless installation that will make your space feel cozy and inviting.

Hardwood

Bring timeless charm to your home with our hardwood installation service. We install quality hardwood floors that add warmth, character, and lasting beauty to every room. Let us help you create a cozy, inviting space with craftsmanship you can trust.

Baseboards

Need new baseboards or want to replace old ones? Our baseboard services provide expert installation and removal, ensuring a perfect fit for any room. Whether you're updating your space or starting a renovation, our team works efficiently to deliver high-quality results with minimal disruption. Trust us to add the finishing touch to your home with precision and care.

Tile Demo

Looking to remove old tiles? Our tile demolition service offers fast, efficient, and clean removal of tiles from any surface, whether it's for a renovation or a complete remodel. We handle everything from floor to wall tiles, ensuring minimal mess and maximum care. Trust our experienced team to get the job done safely and professionally, leaving your space ready for the next step in your project.
